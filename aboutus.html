About Us

Please read about our storied history below

1971-1980

In 1971, our captain Robert Fishman had a vision of opening a world-class seafood restaurant during a fever dream after drinking too much seawater. The SS L'océan was opened to a crowd of the captain's uncle, and quickly became to the go-to spot for delicious meals when there were literally no other options around.

1981-1990

The SS L'océan began the "Catch Your Own Meal" program, becoming our most popular attraction right after the "Help Us Find Our Way to Land We Miss Our Families" program.

1991-2000

Unfortunately, all records of this time have been lost to the sea. Did our head chef continue to expand on our menu of wonderful delicacies? Did the restaurant staff successfully summon Xal'Teth, chaos lord of the sea? Only the waves know the truth.

2001-2010

In order to continue our tradition of creating exciting food experiences, SS L'océan patrons could now take part in our "Pirate Takeover" events. This definitely staged performance allowed guests to help defend the ship from pirates angry at our food being too delicious

2011-2019

Nearing our 50th anniversary, the SS L'océan continues to be proud to serve the people that are able to find us. With some considering us "the best and only restaurant in the Atlantic Ocean", the SS L'océan continues to realize our founder's vision of being a world-class seafood restaurant.
